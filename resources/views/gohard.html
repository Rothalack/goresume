Operation Fragments Funnel

This project was a massive undertaking, a feat of coordination and strategy that pushed the limits of what was possible on Classic WoW servers. For weeks, we worked closely with several major guilds to establish a system for funneling Silithid Carapace Fragments, a key resource required to obtain the extremely exclusive and sought-after AQ mount, the Black Qiraji Battle Tank.

To put it in perspective: each character needed 18,000 fragments to earn the mount. This wasn't a private eventâ€”it was an open-world competition, with the entire server vying for the same prize. We had to fully control the zone, constantly outmaneuvering anyone else who tried to gather fragments.

Our efforts paid off. The system we built was so efficient that we were the first in the world to complete the farming process, enabling us to advance in the quest chain and secure the Scepter Shards, solidifying our place at the forefront of the race.
